Chapter 1

Molly has been staring out of her window for about an hour now.on her desk, lying between copies of Nature, , and all other scientific journal her work had appeared in, was a well thumbed copy of on the road.it had been molly's favorite book since college, and the longer she spent in this four walls the more she felt she needed to be free.

She spent her last ten years in this room, sitting under a poster with oscar wilde quote proclaiming that work is the refuge of people who have nothing better to do. Although many considered her pioneering work, unraveling the secret of the llama DNA, to be an outstanding achievement, Molly did think she had something better to do.
